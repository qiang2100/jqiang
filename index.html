Brief Biography

I am currently assistant Professor at Yangzhou University, Yangzhou, Jiangsu, China. I obtained my PhD degree in November 2016 from Hefei University of Technology (HFUT), under the supervision of Prof. Xindong Wu. I was a visiting Ph.D. student in University of Massachusetts Boston from October 2014 to June 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Ping Chen and Prof. Wei Ding.

Research Interests

  • data mining, machine learning, probabilistic graphical model, text mining
